THE ORIGINAL GOOD SERVANTS N.G.O INTERNATIONAL

OUR OBJECTIVES AND PURPOSE

~ To help educate school dropouts and many who have not set their foot in a class room before and also to help the disable in our community.

~ To help and assist the street children and orphans and also visit the sick in the hospitals and prisoners.

~ To fight for the right of women and children.

~ To assist single parents who can't fend for their wards.

~ To help in the developmental projects in the rural areas, such as constructing water bore holes,toilet facilities, library ect..

~ Establishing training centers to help teens acquire or develop a personal skill on a job or a trade.

~ Distribution of clothes, shoes, and free medical care for the poor and aged.

~ To propagate the gospel of Jesus Christ, Bible distribution and Christian literature to people.
